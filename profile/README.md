# GeothermalArrays

Scaling geothermal energy through networked well arrays.

We develop GPU-accelerated, fully three-dimensional models of deep borehole
heat exchanger (DBHE) arrays, built in [Julia](https://julialang.org/) at the
MIT Julia Lab. Our work spans high-performance scientific computing, geothermal
engineering, and the economics of scaling firm, clean baseload power.
